how it’s made?

Milk is the main ingredient in production of ice-cream and milkshakes. Its quality is the primary factor determining the quality of our milk products.

In order to make sure that our cows give the best milk they are provided with supplemental feed consisting of mixed ration of baled corn residue and distillers’ grains as well as organic grass makes a very good ration.

Samples of milk are examined every day.
